Aegis R0 – Rights Model

Your Shield in Every Situation

🌍 Purpose

The Aegis R0 “Rights” model is designed to provide ultra-affordable, rugged smartphones for vulnerable communities, activists, and those in need of digital protection. Priced at just $130–150, it's your personal shield in hostile times.

📱 Key Features

  • Rugged polycarbonate chassis (IP68 / MIL-STD-810H)
  • 12MP rear camera + 5MP front camera (LED next to camera)
  • Dual independent buttons: PTT + Action
  • Digital Stealth Mode, secure comms features
  • 5000mAh battery
  • DSSS antenna support for basic local encrypted communication
  • Runs Aegis OS (Android-based with privacy-first customizations)
